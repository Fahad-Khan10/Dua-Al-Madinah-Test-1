ABOUT US

“Duaa al Madinah ." Is founded in 2020, started by very young personalities to dedicate their life spending time on doing assistance to guest of Allah. Our objective is to provide solutions for umrah, Haj and Ziyarat services to our clients in Saudi Arabia and other countries. Our consultative approach and intelligent proprietary technology systems will help drive better outcomes. The company is committed to offer wide range of Umrah, Haj & Ziyarat services that includes, flight ticketing, hotel reservation, documents attestation, transportation services, individual/group packages pilgrimage tours and ground handling services. and all travel related services.

Duaa al Madinah provides following types of services:

  • Hajj, Umrah and Ziyarat Services
  • Flight Bookings
  • Hotel Bookings
  • Catering Services
